Features

Everything your wardrobe needs. Nothing it doesn't.

BURS is built around one idea: your best outfits already live in your closet.

01

Live Garment Scan

Point your camera at one garment. BURS reads color, fabric and silhouette live in under 2 seconds, without turning your wardrobe into a tagging chore.

  • Live scan one garment in under 2 seconds
  • Computer vision reads the details for you
  • No manual tagging, spreadsheets or uploads to a marketplace
02

Outfit of the Day

Every morning, BURS reads the weather, your calendar and your location context before suggesting one confident look from pieces you already own.

  • Weather-aware layers and fabrics
  • Calendar-aware outfit intent
  • One clear recommendation instead of a feed
03

AI Stylist Chat

Ask BURS to make an outfit warmer, sharper, softer or more relaxed. The chat understands your wardrobe and swaps pieces conversationally.

  • Refine any look in plain language
  • Swap pieces without starting over
  • Ask styling questions against your real closet
04

Calendar Sync

Connect Google Calendar or ICS and BURS plans around what is actually coming up. Your week gets dressed before it starts.

  • Google Calendar and ICS support
  • Event-aware outfit planning
  • Fewer last-minute wardrobe decisions
05

Travel Capsule

Tell BURS where you are going and for how long. It builds a minimum viable packing list from your own wardrobe so every piece earns its place.

  • Pack fewer pieces with more outfit coverage
  • Match weather, location and trip length
  • Build capsules from clothes you already own
06

Week Planner

Plan seven days of outfits in one pass. BURS understands events, repeats and gaps so your wardrobe is ready before Monday morning.

  • Seven-day outfit planning
  • Event-aware recommendations
  • Reduce decision fatigue all week
07

Wardrobe Gaps

BURS identifies what is missing based on your lifestyle and outfit coverage. It shows the gaps that would unlock more looks, not impulse buys.

  • Understand missing categories and layers
  • See gaps through real outfit coverage
  • Shop less often and more precisely
08

Mood Outfit

Tell BURS how you want to feel and get dressed accordingly. Confident, calm, creative or understated becomes a wardrobe direction.

  • Dress from mood, not only occasion
  • Translate feeling into pieces and palette
  • Save mood-led looks for later
09

Ghost Mannequin

Visualise outfits on a neutral body form without needing a mirror or camera. It keeps the focus on proportion, layers and outfit balance.

  • Preview outfit shape quickly
  • No mirror photo required
  • Compare silhouettes before getting dressed
10

Wardrobe Insights & Analytics

See what you wear, what you ignore and what earns its space. BURS turns usage, cost-per-wear and underused pieces into decisions you can act on.

  • Track most-worn and underused items
  • Understand cost-per-wear over time
  • Keep only what works for your life
11

Style DNA

Your Style DNA is a personal style fingerprint built from your scan and outfit feedback over time. BURS learns what you actually choose, not what a trend says you should wear.

  • Build a private style profile from real behavior
  • Improve recommendations with feedback
  • Make your wardrobe feel more like you
