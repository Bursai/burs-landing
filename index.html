What is BURS?

BURS is an AI wardrobe stylist. It scans the clothes you already own, reads the weather and your calendar, and recommends the perfect outfit for every day.

How does the wardrobe scan work?

You point your phone camera at a garment. BURS reads colour, fabric and silhouette live in under 2 seconds, then adds it to your wardrobe. No manual tagging, no spreadsheets, no uploading to a marketplace.

Is BURS free?

BURS is a premium app — 119 kr/month or 899 kr/year. No free tier, no ads. Cancel anytime from your account settings.

Does BURS work on iPhone and Android?

BURS ships on iOS and Android with an identical experience. Your wardrobe syncs across every device you sign in on, so the outfit you planned on your phone is waiting on your tablet.

Will BURS suggest clothes I do not own?

No. BURS is built around the principle that your best outfits already live in your closet. It only recommends pieces already in your wardrobe — the opposite of a shopping app. You never get pushed into buying anything.

How does BURS handle my photos and data?

Your wardrobe photos stay private to your account. BURS does not sell your data and does not train public models on your images. You can delete every piece and every photo from inside the app at any time.
